Training & Education

I am a passionate educator with over 10 years of experience facilitating workshops, presentations, and trainings on topics relating to mental health, gender, and sexuality.
Contact me today to book a training for your organization or community group!

Offerings include:

For mental health professionals
  • Foundations of Self-of-the-Therapist Work
  • Polyamory and Consensual Non-Monogamy in the Therapy Room
  • LGBTQ+ Identity Development across the Lifespan
  • Affirmative Care for LGBTQ+ Youth and their Families
  • Working with Transgender Clients
  • Recognizing and Unlearning Therapist Bias
For general audiences & organizations
  • Understanding Transgender and Gender Expansive Identities
  • Creating LGBTQ+ Welcoming Spaces
  • Supporting Transgender Youth

I am open to creating a unique training beyond the topics listed above. If you are interested in partnering on a bespoke workshop to meet your participants' specific needs, please reach out to start a conversation!
